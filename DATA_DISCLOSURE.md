## Dataset

The project uses data from the NYU fastMRI Initiative:

```text
https://fastmri.med.nyu.edu/
```

The prostate MRI dataset contains MRI exams acquired on 3 Tesla scanners, including axial T2-weighted and axial diffusion-weighted images.

## Access And Redistribution

The NYU fastMRI website states that access requires acceptance of the Dataset Sharing Agreement and an individual application. It also states that fastMRI data may be used for internal research or educational purposes only and may not be redistributed without prior permission.

For that reason:

- This GitHub-ready folder does **not** include raw DICOM files.
- This GitHub-ready folder should not include fastMRI download links.
- This GitHub-ready folder should not include patient images or other redistributable copies of fastMRI data.
- Team members or reviewers who need the dataset should access it directly through NYU fastMRI and comply with its Dataset Sharing Agreement.

## Clinical Disclaimer

This is a research and educational prototype. It is not approved for clinical use and must not be used for diagnosis or patient care.
